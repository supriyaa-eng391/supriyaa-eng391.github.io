Res-Q

A multilingual emergency assistant that helps people get guidance in crisis situations. It supports conversations in over 70 languages and provides real-time voice responses. The system can also detect a user’s location and provide relevant information such as nearby hospitals, weather conditions, and safety alerts.

PennyWise

A budgeting platform designed to help students manage their finances more easily. It allows users to track spending, manage loans, and understand their financial habits. Built during a 24-hour hackathon with a team of six, the project won the “Best Beginner Hack” award at Hack(H)er413 2025.
